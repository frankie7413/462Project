Student Feedback

What is Student Feedback? Student Feedback is a service that helps shield students from ever going out of their comfort zones to talk to proffesors. This way you can keep your introvertness save from ever having to eventually talk to proffesor even if you do have a good point or feedback to give.

Student Here Professor Here

Why give Feedback?

Want to tell professor how much you enjoyed the lecture? Want to provide advice on how to improve the class? Want to ask to go over material in more detail? Want to ask for more resourses on material? Want to ask questions based on career goals? We can foward questions, concerns, and feedback to your professor Studentymously. Furthermore, if you want to receive a reply only to oneself, we provide the option to get the reply from professor and keep one Studentymous. Otherwise, the professor can elect to reply to whole class if they find your message helpful to the current class.
